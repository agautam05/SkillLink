Frequently Asked Questions

How does SkillLink connect students with recruiters?

SkillLink uses AI-powered matching algorithms to connect students with relevant recruiters based on skills, interests, and career goals. Our platform facilitates meaningful connections through personalized recommendations and direct messaging features.

How do I access my reports?

You can access your reports by navigating to the Reports section in your dashboard. From there, you can view detailed analytics on your profile views, skill assessments, and connection statistics. Reports can be filtered by date range and exported for your records.

Can I message recruiters directly?

Yes, SkillLink allows direct messaging between students and recruiters. Once you've established a connection, you can communicate through our secure messaging platform to discuss opportunities, ask questions, and schedule interviews.

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile, go to your dashboard and click on the "Edit Profile" button. From there, you can modify your personal information, skills, education, work experience, and portfolio items. Remember to save your changes before exiting.

Is my data secure on SkillLink?

Absolutely. We take data security seriously. SkillLink uses industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your personal information. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent, and you have full control over your privacy settings.

How can I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to Settings > Account Settings > Delete Account. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our systems in accordance with our data retention policies.
